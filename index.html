About Me

Hello, I'm a 21-year-old game developer from Poland. I've been programming since around age 13. I started out making silly programs in school which would do things like open the broswer a million times, or play loud beeping noises. However shortly after, I switched over to creating games on Roblox Studio, I became obsessed and spent a whole year working for free for anybody I could find because of the passion I have for creating games & programming.

Over the years I've worked on hundreds of unique game systems ranging from cars, light sabers, laser guns, flying machines, gravity simulations, self learning algorithms, databases, user interfaces, and more.

I am capable of learning new things quickly as I believe I have a natural intuition when it comes to programming, I just understand it. I am capable of doing things outside my scope of knowledge with minimal training. In my free time I like to research other fields of programming which I've never done, which gives my knowledge a lot of overlap.
